Hi 👋 My name is Gunasekhar Jenni
=================================

AI Engineer
-----------

I'm Gunasekhar, an AI Engineer and M.Sc. Computer Science graduate from the University of Paderborn, Germany. Over 2 years as a Research Assistant at Fraunhofer IEE, I worked on large-scale energy datasets, geospatial analysis, and multimodal AI systems. My Master's thesis pushed me deep into the frontier of Vision-Language Models, evaluating open-source VLMs to classify urban infrastructure from aerial imagery and linking raw pixels to real-world geospatial databases.

I build things end-to-end. From RAG pipelines and LLM-powered document generation tools to full-stack AI applications and explainability frameworks for Graph Neural Networks, I have designed architectures, shipped features, and worn multiple hats across research and engineering. My stack centres around Python, LangChain, HuggingFace, PyTorch, FastAPI, and PostgreSQL, but I pick up whatever the problem demands.

Beyond the code, I am an explorer by nature, drawn to open questions, emerging tools, and domains where AI is just beginning to make an impact. I enjoy breaking down complex ideas and sharing them, having organised workshops on Generative AI and open-source LLMs for the Google Developer Group in Paderborn.

Currently seeking AI/ML Engineering opportunities. Always open to interesting problems and meaningful collaborations.
